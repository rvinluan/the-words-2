How to play

Type in a word, then hit enter. That word will then fall into the grid.

The word doesn't have to be 5 letters long, it can be longer or shorter.

If you have 3 or more of a letter on the board, you can click one of them to clear them.

You lose if the board fills up past the top.

The purple line at the bottom indicates which column is the special column. When you enter a word, the letter that is inside this column will turn purple.

When you clear a purple letter off the board, it goes into your bank (in the top left).

If you can collect all 26 letters in your bank, you win!

Good luck!

Hard Mode

This mode has only two differences from normal mode:

1) You can only clear letters off the board if they are in a contiguous block not just anywhere on the board. Diagonals count.

2) If you can get a group of 4 or more letters, you will clear bonus adjacent tiles in addition to those letters.

Good luck!
