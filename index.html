How to play

Type in a word, then hit enter. That word will then fall into the grid.

The word doesn't have to be 5 letters long, it can be longer or shorter.

If you have 3 or more of a letter on the board, you can click one of them to clear them.

You lose if the board fills up past the top.

The glowing square indicates that a letter entered there will turn the special color.

When you clear a special letter off the board, it goes into your bank (in the top left).

If you can collect all 26 letters in your bank, you win!

Good luck!

Hard Mode

This mode has only two differences from normal mode:

1) You can only clear letters off the board if they are in a contiguous block not just anywhere on the board. Diagonals count.

2) If you can get a group of 4 or more letters, you will clear bonus adjacent tiles in addition to those letters.

Good luck!

Puzzle Mode

In this mode, click the words to add them to the board. Your goal is still to complete the bank of letters. If you can get all the special letters, you win!

Clear Mode

In this mode, click the words to add them to the board. Your goal is to clear the board of all letters. Remember that letters must be contiguous to clear them. Special letters don't matter in this mode. Good luck!

Waterfall Mode

In this mode, click the words to add them to the board. Your goal is to play all the predefined words in a way that clears the board. Special letters don't matter in this mode, but entered words will now start from the glowing square instead of from the leftmost edge of the board. Good luck!
